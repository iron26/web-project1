Iron Uriostegui's biography





Iron Uriostegui (february 17 1985, Toluca, Mexico)

He was born and raised in the city of Toluca, Mexico, where he finished elementary school and attended high school while pursuing a technical career in basic computer science.
At the age of 15 he changed his place of residence to the United States of North America where he studied the preparatory school in the city of Palatine State of Illinois
In the United States he had different jobs such as chef and cashier in restaurant

Iron is currently studying software development engineering at Hola Code Mexico City where he hopes to excel and find a well-paid job in Mexico or the United States.
